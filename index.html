How It Works
Roll 4 Focus is a gamified Pomodoro timer. Roll dice to set Hope, Fear, and Sound themes for each focus session. Complete sessions to earn Inspiration, trigger battles against Bosses and Minions, and unlock treasure chests. Build streaks by completing consecutive sessions!

User Manual
Overview▾

Roll 4 Focus is a dice-themed focus timer. You roll dice to set a session length, work the session, and earn Inspiration, Boss and Minion battles, and chests. Completing sessions back-to-back builds a streak.

Everything lives on three tabs: Home (timer, battles, dice stats), Logs (history), and Settings (⚙). All data is stored in your browser — nothing is uploaded.

Session Timer▾

The ring in the center counts your session down. The time in the middle is an editable MM:SS field — tap it (while stopped) to edit, using any of five methods:

TypeEnter digits directly. Clamps to range when you leave the field.
▴ ▾ clickStep the value ±1. Wraps past the ends.
▴ ▾ holdAuto-repeats, accelerating, until you let go.
WheelScroll over a field to snap to the nearest 5.
↑ ↓ keysStep ±1 while a field is focused.

Controls below the ring: ▶ / ⏸ start & pause, ↺ reset to the rolled time, ☕ open the break timer. The duration locks while the timer runs. The session-start sound only plays on a fresh start, not on resume.

When the timer hits zero you're asked "Session Success?" — answer Yes or No (or press Y/N). Success awards Inspiration, earns a Boss battle, and extends your streak; failure resets the streak to 0.

Break Timer▾

The ☕ control opens a separate break timer. Set a duration with the same MM:SS editor (default 15:00), then ▶ Start. It runs independently of the session timer. ↺ resets it, ✕ closes. Optional start/finish sounds can be enabled in Settings.

Dice & Rolls▾

Each session uses three dice: Hope (HP), Fear (FR), and Sound (SD). 🎲 Roll for Me rolls all three at once; the soundtrack reroll (↻) rerolls just the Sound die. You can also tap a die chip in the stats strip to enter rolls by hand.

Session length is derived from the roll plus your Base Time setting (default 10 min):

Hope = FearLength = Hope + Base Time, and you gain +1 Inspiration.
OtherwiseLength = max(Hope, Fear) + Base Time.

The Sound roll maps to a row in your Soundtrack Table (Settings) and mirrors the Hope value unless you set it yourself. Rolls are tagged auto (rolled by the app) or manual (entered by you); manual rolls can be attributed to a named die via Dice Names.

Inspiration & Penalty▾

Inspiration (INSP) lowers every battle DC; Penalty (PEN) raises it. Both show in the stats strip and persist until reset. Tap either chip to adjust by hand.

Inspiration is awarded on session success, scaling with length:

< 30 min+1
30–34 min+2
35–39 min+3
40+ min+4

You also gain +1 on a Hope = Fear roll and on a Boss crit, and lose 1 on a Minion/Boss fumble (nat 1).

Boss & Minion Battles▾

Completing sessions and logging time earns battles, shown as QUEUED on each card. Attack by rolling a d20 against the card's DC:

Roll ≥ DCWin — earns a chest. Boss DC scales up (see DC Scaling); Minion DC +1.
Nat 20Crit — win + +1 Inspiration.
Nat 1Fumble — miss + −1 Inspiration.
Roll < DCMiss — no chest.

The ⚔ ATK button auto-rolls; ♦ LOOT opens one chest. Clicking anywhere else on a card opens its Manual Entry modal, where you type the result of a real die roll. Base DCs default to Boss 24 / Minion 18 (Settings).

Boss DC Scaling▾

Each Boss win raises the Boss DC. The increase shrinks as the DC climbs, so a hard boss ramps slowly and an easy one ramps fast. The Boss DC Scaling (Max) setting is the largest step (at low DC); the rest derive from it, flooring at +1 once DC reaches 20. With the default Max of 5:

DC 1–4+5
DC 5–9+4
DC 10–14+3
DC 15–19+2
DC 20++1

The band is read from the effective DC (after Inspiration and Penalty), so banking Inspiration both lowers the DC and makes it ramp faster. Minion DC always rises a flat +1 per win.

Chests & Carryover▾

Every battle win earns a chest. Open them with the ♦ LOOT button on the card. The card shows chests remaining.

When you press ↺ Reset, any unopened chests carry forward into the new period as battles — Boss chests become Boss battles, Minion chests become Minion battles. Everything else (Inspiration, Penalty, streak, counts) clears. Your best streak is kept.

Manual Entry▾

The Boss/Minion Manual Entry modal is for entering real die rolls. Type the d20 result and press ⚔ Attack (or Enter). Steppers adjust DC, Battles, and Chests. The // Last Roll line confirms each result without a pop-up.

Turn on Keep Open (header) to enter rolls back-to-back. When one side's queue empties, it auto-switches to the other side if battles remain there; when both are empty it closes with a "Queue empty" cue. Press B / M to open Boss / Minion entry directly.

Minion Countdown & Time▾

While a session runs, the Minion card counts down to the next earned battle. You earn one Minion battle per Battle Interval minutes of session time (default 10). Enable Auto-Fight (Settings) to resolve earned Minion battles automatically.

+ Add Session Time (Adventuring Time card) logs time done outside the timer. Logged time earns Minion battles by the same interval, Boss battles per the Manual Entry — Boss Battle minutes (default 20), and +1 Inspiration per Boss battle earned.

Dice Stats▾

The Dice Stats card breaks down your rolls for a chosen period (Today / 7d / Month / All, with ‹ › to step back through periods). It has three sections:

Top RollsFace-frequency bars for your most-rolled dice (d20 battles only). Nat 1 red, nat 20 lime.
Roll AveragesAverage per die (Auto + named dice) across Boss / Minion / All.
Most RolledMost-frequent Hope / Fear / Sound from your sessions.

The Filter dropdown limits which dice (including Auto) appear in Top Rolls and Roll Averages. Unnamed manual rolls aren't shown here — name your die in Dice Names to track it.

Logs▾

The Logs tab has two sub-tabs. Battles summarizes Total / Win / Loss / Nat 20; Sessions summarizes Sess / Done / Min / Avg. Each row shows the roll and outcome; the ✎ button edits a row, with a ⌫ Delete inside the editor.

Each sub-tab has Clear All, Export CSV, and Import CSV. Imported rows append and re-sort by time.

Adventuring Time▾

Shows Today and This Week totals (timed sessions + logged time), a battle-stats grid (Earned / Fought / Won / Remaining / Chests earned-opened-remaining for Boss and Minion), and your Streak (current and best).

Settings▾

Grouped into colored sections:

ConfigurationTheme (Classic / Arcade), config export/import, embed sounds, reset.
TimerBase Time (10), Boss DC (24), Minion DC (18), Hope/Fear Die (d20), Soundtrack Die (d20).
SoundPer-sound toggles + custom file uploads (see Sounds).
MinionCountdown on/off, Battle Interval (10), Auto-Fight.
BossDC Scaling Max (5), Manual Entry minutes per Boss battle (20).
Dice NamesManage the named dice used to tag manual rolls.
Soundtrack20-row table mapping each Sound roll to a soundtrack.
Sounds & Quiet Mode▾

Most events have an optional sound (start, dice roll, attack hit/miss, chest, inspiration, break start/finish, manual-entry close). Each can be toggled and given a custom .mp3 / .wav in Sound Settings. Manual-Entry Close and Minion Countdown are on by default; most others are off.

The 🔊 / 🔇 header button (or Q) toggles Quiet Mode, which mutes everything (the session-end chime uses a quieter variant).

Saving, Data & Shortcuts▾

Everything saves automatically to your browser's local storage — settings, logs, counters, custom sounds, and your theme. Use Export / Import Config (Settings) to move your setup between browsers or back it up. Theme is stored separately, so importing a config won't change it.

For keyboard shortcuts, press the ? button in the header anytime.
